part one
the accidental
beginning
wrong screen, right person

i opened the app

not looking for anything

the way you don't look for things

right before you find them

the screen blinked

and there you were

a stranger with the energy of someone

i'd already been talking to my whole life

it was stupid

it was ometv at a weird hour

it was exactly right

you can feel it through the screen

everybody looking

nobody saying what for

and then you said something

i don't even remember what

but i laughed before i could stop myself

that's how it starts, i think —

not slowly

but suddenly, and then all at once

and now a year later

here we are

the strangest friends

less careful

more true
